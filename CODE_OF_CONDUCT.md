# Code of Conduct

Switcher is an open-source project, and participation should be respectful, constructive, and focused on improving the software.

## Expected behavior

- Be considerate of different experience levels and backgrounds.
- Give actionable, technical feedback.
- Keep discussions on topic.
- Assume good intent, but accept corrections when behavior creates a problem.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or behavior.
- Posting private information without consent.
- Repeated off-topic, disruptive, or bad-faith comments.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove comments, close issues, reject pull requests, or restrict participation when conduct harms the project or its contributors.

To report a conduct issue, contact the maintainer privately at Kshitizsuwal@gmail.com. Reports will be handled with discretion.
